Our Name
SUCCESSORS

Last year marked the introduction of STEM Racing to GEMS Founders School, where the first team, Team Founders, excelled in the Professional category, securing 6th place alongside two prestigious awards: Best Pit Display and Best Verbal Presentation. Yet, despite these achievements, their journey remained unfinished. Driven by a shared ambition to represent GFS and the UAE on the international stage, Team Successors was born with clear purpose and intent. Our identity is centred on the legacy we inherit, not simply to follow it, but to advance it. We carry forward the responsibility of completing what was left unfinished: to become national champions and raise the UAE flag on the stage of the world's most prestigious STEM competition.

Chosen for its dual meaning: legacy carried forward with clear momentum, progression, and, leadership.

Values & Identity
Inheriting the Legacy. Defining the Future.

Our motto is a reflection of what Team Successors stands for: respecting the work established by the inaugural team whilst pushing ourselves to set a new standard. For us, it is about progress, not imitation.

Growth Mindset Our mindset revolves around continuous and progressive improvement, as we seek to constantly elevate our standards as a team.
Responsibility We acknowledge the responsibility and sense of accountability that stems from inheriting the school's identity and legacy.
Innovation We challenge ourselves to think differently, solve problems creatively, and keep improving beyond expectations throughout our work.
Ambition We have always sought to create a new benchmark for what a STEM Racing team is capable of, both within the UAE and internationally.

Logo Development

We looked at different Formula 1 circuits and combined multiple sections from different tracks to construct a crown-shaped logo.

02

03

The final logo combines track-based geometry, intentional incompleteness, and a clear crown silhouette into one cohesive identity.

Logo - Detailed Breakdown
F1 circuit fragments composing the crown silhouette

The final Team Successors logo transforms a crown into a constructed symbol made from fragments of iconic Formula 1 circuits, including Monza, Austin, Marina Bay, Losail, Imola, Silverstone, Yas Marina, Sakhir, and, Mexico. This gives the logo two meanings at once: a crown representing ambition and succession, and a track-based structure that directly connects our identity to our passion for F1. It is intentionally incomplete in its geometry to place emphasis on the unfinished mission we aspire to fulfil: becoming national champions.
