Who We Are

We are the GEMS Founders School F1 in Schools team, built to carry forward an unfinished mission. After the inaugural team secured 6th place and two category awards, our aim became clear: complete the story by becoming national champions and raising the UAE flag on the world’s most prestigious STEM stage.

Our Sponsors
Pit Stop · 01 Team & Sponsor Outlook

Our sponsors receive more than logo placement on a car. They are integrated into a focused marketing and outreach system built around a high-performance story, giving them visibility, trust, and measurable impact across activations, content, and the national finals race window.

Pit Stop · 02 Event Planning

To guide delivery, we framed the project scope statement as a contract that defined objectives, deliverables, constraints, and ownership. This helped us maintain quality across event planning, with sponsor tier shaping the scale of each activation and its outreach. These included International Day for all sponsors, alongside an Ethara collaboration and an AI-driven workshop that connected our turbo sponsor, Spicebox, with future engineers.

Pit Stop · 03 CAD Design

Our design philosophy centres on identifying the limits of the technical regulations and using aerodynamic principles to exploit the most effective areas within them. Fusion 360, ANSYS Discovery, and TurboFlow support an iterative process from early geometry to validated airflow.

Pit Stop · 04 Events

Showcasing our brand, sponsor network, and engineering journey to more than 3,000 attendees and school stakeholders.

Pit Stop · 05 Car Manufacturing

Before 3D printing, measured tolerance is added to allow for sanding while maintaining accuracy. The CAD file is prepared in UltiMaker Cura with adaptive layers and a low layer height to achieve smoother curved surfaces. After printing, the model is sanded from 200-grit to 1000-grit along the direction of airflow.
